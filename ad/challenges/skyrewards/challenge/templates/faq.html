Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points?

Currently, points are awarded upon account creation (10,000 pts). We are working on flight integration.

Can I transfer points to family?

Yes! Use the dashboard to instantly transfer points to anyone!

Why did my points disappear?

To ensure fairness and prevent inflation, our automated system resets accounts that hold excessive unverified balances (over 2,000,000). Also, purchasing VIP items may require emptying your account balance as part of the transaction cost.

How do I get Platinum Status?

Platinum status grants a code to the exclusive lounge. It costs 1,000,000 points. This seems a little high but we are sure you'll find a way.
