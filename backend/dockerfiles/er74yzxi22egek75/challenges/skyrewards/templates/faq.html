Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points?

Currently, points are awarded upon account creation (10,000 pts). We are working on flight integration.

Can I transfer points to family?

Yes! Use the dashboard to instantly transfer points to any other registered username.

Why did my points disappear?

To ensure fairness and prevent inflation, our automated system resets accounts that hold excessive unverified balances (over 500k). Also, purchasing VIP items may require emptying your account balance as part of the transaction cost.

How do I get Platinum Status?

Platinum status allows access to the Flag Lounge. It costs 1,000,000 points. Good luck saving up!
